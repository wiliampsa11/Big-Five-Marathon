Rules & Regulations

To ensure a fair and safe race for everyone, please keep these rules in mind:

  • Change of Distance: Switching distances during the race will result in an FNT (finished no time). Changes before race day will be officially registered.
  • Top Finishers: The first three male and female finishers are determined by who crosses the finish line first, not net time.
  • Gear: Nordic walking and trekking poles are not permitted.
  • Safety: Runner safety is our top priority. We may pick up runners if necessary for their well-being.

Medical Information

Your health matters. All participants are responsible for their own travel insurance.

On-site Medical Assistance:

  • English-speaking Danish doctors and South African paramedics will be present throughout the event, ready to assist with injuries or exhaustion.
  • Albatros Adventure Marathons ensures a qualified medical team is available. If further treatment is needed at local facilities, neither the medical team nor the travel agency is liable for external care.
